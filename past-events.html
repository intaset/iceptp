Past Events

The previous event in this conference series was held on April 8 - 10, 2018, in Budapest, Hungary. ICEPTP, previously known as ICESDP and AWSPT, is one of the conferences that make up the World Congress on Civil, Structural, and Environmental Engineering (CSEE). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the congress becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Sidika Aksoy, for the paper entitled: "Effect of Water Quality Values on Gammaridae and Ephemeroptera Biodiversity in Mezit River (Bilecik-Turkey)";

  • Pali Bhave, for the paper entitled: "Effect of Saturation on Dynamic Soil Properties in Low Strain Range Using Resonant Column Apparatus";

  • Javier Rodriguez Corral, for the paper entitled: " Agarose Gel as a Soil Analogue for Novel Ground Improvement Applications ";

  • Danish Farooq, for the paper entitled: "Analysis of Young Driver Behaviour related to Road Safety Issues in Pakistan and Hungary";

  • Dr. Yaser Hawas, for the paper entitled: "Comparative Performance Analysis of Multi-Objective Transit-Priority Systems";

  • Dr. Stefan Jung, for the paper entitled: " Deep Improvement of Heterogeneous Subsoils under Urban Conditions ";

  • Jiwon Lee, for the paper entitled: "Relative Humidity Prediction Model with Missing Data Refinement Using a Long Short-Term Memory Neural Network";

  • Dr. M. Shamim Miah, for the paper entitled: " Optimal and Suboptimal Vibration Control of Structures ";

  • Dr. Meysam Majidi Nezhad, for the paper entitled: 'Oil Spill Detection Analyzing “Sentinel 2” Satellite Images: A Persian Gulf Case Study';

  • Nina Roettgers, for the paper entitled: "H2O-CC Combination of Reverse-Osmosis and Photovoltaic";

  • Dr. Sami Tabsh, for the paper entitled: "Shear Strength of Reinforced Concrete Beams Made with Recycled Aggregate";

  • Dr. Yen Po Wang, for the paper entitled: " An Experimental Study of In-plane Oval Damper ";

  • Dr. Keisuke Watanabe, for the paper entitled: "A Concept of Coastal Sea Monitoring System from Sky to Water".
